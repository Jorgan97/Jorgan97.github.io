About Me

      Hello there! My name is Joseph Morgan, and I am from the Outer Banks of North Carolina. Having grown up only fifteen minutes away from where the Wright Brothers made their first flights, I have had a life-long fascination with the engineering of everything that flies. This fascination inspired me to study aerospace engineering at North Carolina State University. I have interests in aeronautics, boats, mechanical systems, music, the outdoors, athletics, and other fun things.
